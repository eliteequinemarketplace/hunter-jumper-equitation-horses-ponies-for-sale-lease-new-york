New York and the Tri-State area represent one of the most important and prestigious hunter jumper markets in North America — a region defined by world-class venues, top professional programs, and some of the most competitive show horse buyers in the sport. Elite Equine Marketplace lists hunter jumper horses for sale from New York and Tri-State sellers, connecting buyers directly with horses competing at the region's top venues.

HITS Saugerties — The Northeast's Premier Hunter Jumper Venue

HITS Saugerties, located in the Hudson Valley, is the Northeast's most important hunter jumper competition venue — hosting major shows throughout the summer season that draw top horses and riders from across the East Coast. Horses with HITS Saugerties competition records have proven themselves in one of the most competitive summer circuits in the country. Elite Equine Marketplace lists horses from HITS Saugerties-based trainers and sellers throughout the summer season.

Old Salem Farm & The Spring Classics

Old Salem Farm in North Salem, New York hosts the iconic Old Salem Farm Spring Horse Shows each May — one of the most prestigious and beautiful horse show venues in North America. The Spring Classics draw top equitation horses, hunter horses, and jumper horses from across the country, making Old Salem one of the key venues in the East Coast spring calendar. Old Salem is particularly important for equitation — it is one of the most competitive equitation stops on the East Coast calendar.

The National Horse Show & Indoor Season

The National Horse Show, held each November, is one of the most historic and prestigious horse shows in North America — a key stop on the fall indoor circuit alongside Capital Challenge and WIHS. Horses competing at the National Horse Show carry the prestige of one of the sport's most storied venues, and horses qualified for or recently competed at the indoors are among the most sought-after in the fall market.
